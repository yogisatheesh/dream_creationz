About Dream Creationz

From my Career as a finance manageer, I discovered a true passion for event planning, which led me to pursue my dream of creating unforgettable experiences. This passion became reality with the launch of Dream Creations Event Management, where i bring creativity and precision together to craft exceptional events.

Founded in 2017, Dream Creationsz started with a passion for event decoration and has since grown into a full - service event management company. We currently provide our services across Malaysia, with the vision of explanding world wide. From breathtaking decor to live entertainment, catering, and technical support, we bring every event to life with creativity, excellence and attention to detail.
